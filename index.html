TonyPi Navagation

Goals

  The use of tags to achieve intersection steering:

    We plan to use the tag recognition function of TonyPi, which can automatically identify the corresponding tag and make a left or right turn. In this way, we only need to label the left or right turn at the intersection.

  Promblems:

  1. TonyPi often recognize the label in advance, how to make TonyPi complete the left or right turn at the right time?

  2. The TonyPi often has trouble walking in a straight line, causing it to hit a wall or the tag to be out of the camera's view

  3. TonyPi provides a tutorial for changing the action corresponding to the tag, which is easier to implement
